Honey Glazed Salmon

This succulent dish is one of my all-time favorites. Considering how easy it is to make, this dish offers an astounding complexity of flavor. I suggest using local honey for the added benefit of allergen immunity!

Ingredients

  • 1 filet of salmon
  • 1 cup of honey
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tsp of soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons of water

Steps

  • Pat dry the salmon using paper towels.
  • Add a dash of salt and pepper to each side.
  • Heat oven-safe skillet to medium high with 1 tsp of olive oil, and cook the filet for four minutes, skin-side down.
  • Pre-heat your broiler to high.
  • While the filet is cooking, add honey, garlic, soy sauce, and 4 tablespoons of water.
  • Spoon the sauce over the filet and move the pan into the broiler.
  • Cook for 6-8 minutes, basting the salmon again half-way through.
